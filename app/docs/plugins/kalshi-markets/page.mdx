# Kalshi Markets

Bring prediction markets to your broadcast. Simply copy a market URL from Kalshi and paste it into your dashboard.

## How to Set It Up

<img
  src="/docs/images/plugins/kalshi/how-to-setup.png"
  alt="Kalshi Markets plugin setup"
  style={{
    borderRadius: "8px",
    marginTop: "1rem",

/>

1. Navigate to the **Kalshi Markets** plugin in your dashboard
2. Insert a valid [Kalshi](https://kalshi.com/) URL and click the confirm button
3. Check the preview, set the duration of the popup in the overlay and click the "Add to Overlay" button

- The overlay will show a popup with the market's question, the possible answers and a QR code that redirects to the related page on Kalshi.

## Visible answers

> **Warning:** A maximum of 3 markets are shown in the overlay popup, even if there may be more related to the same question
