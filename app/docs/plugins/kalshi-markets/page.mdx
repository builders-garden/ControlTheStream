# Kalshi Markets

Bring prediction markets to your broadcast. Simply copy a market URL from Kalshi and paste it into your dashboard.

## How It Works

1. Find a market on Kalshi that you want to feature
2. Copy the market URL
3. Paste it into your ControlTheStream dashboard
4. The market data will appear on your overlay

## Features

- Displays market data on your overlay
- Shows QR code redirecting viewers to Kalshi web app
- Viewers can place their bets directly
- Real-time market updates
