# Configure Plugins via Dashboard

Your dashboard is your customization hub. ControlTheStream offers five powerful plugins to drive engagement and monetization.<br/>
You can activate and configure these to determine how they appear on your OBS overlay.

## Available Plugins

| Plugin | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| [Tipping](/docs/plugins/tipping) | Enable your audience to support you directly with USDC tips |
| [Pyro](/docs/plugins/pyro) | Let viewers sponsor your stream by burning ETH on Base |
| [Bull Meter](/docs/plugins/bull-meter) | Create interactive, skin-in-the-game polls |
| [Kalshi Markets](/docs/plugins/kalshi-markets) | Bring prediction markets to your broadcast |
| [Token Highlight](/docs/plugins/token-highlight) | Feature up to 6 tokens for your community |
| [Creator Coin](/docs/plugins/creator-coin) | Highlight a Zora Creator Token |

Click on any plugin above to learn more about how to configure and use it.
