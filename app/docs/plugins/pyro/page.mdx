# Pyro

Let your audience sponsor your stream by burning your token!<br/>
Powered by [Pyro](https://www.pyro.buzz), this plugin enables a burn-to-advertise system where sponsors compete on a leaderboard to be featured in your streams.

## How to Set It Up

1. Navigate to the **Pyro** plugin in your dashboard
2. Enter the email address associated with your Pyro account
3. Click "Send Code" to receive a verification code via email
4. Enter the 6-digit OTP code to verify and connect your account
5. Once connected, your sponsor leaderboard will appear in the dashboard

> **Note:** You need an existing [Pyro](https://www.pyro.buzz) creator account to use this plugin.

## Disconnecting

You can disconnect your Pyro account at any time from the dashboard. Your leaderboard data will be preserved even after disconnecting.
